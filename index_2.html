What is Metaverse？

Metaverse is a decentralized project which is based on blockchain. We are going to establish a digital asset web and provide service framework of the Digital Identity Verification and Oracle on the blockchain. Metaverse will be an open system of the flow of the digital value. As the only blockchain project based on POW in China，Metaverse provides the features of publishing the digital assets、transfering the digital assets、registering the digital identity and Oracle and data abstract.
